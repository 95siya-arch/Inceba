Who We Are

A Trusted Name in Funeral Services

"Phakathi kokudliwa kobomi sikwasekufeni"

Inceba Funeral Palour has been serving families across KwaZulu-Natal and the Eastern Cape since 2011. We understand that losing a loved one is one of life's most difficult moments.

Led by Director Lusindiso Mhlwatika, our dedicated team of branch managers ensures that every family receives dignified, compassionate care — no matter where they are.

From repatriation services to décor for any event, we handle every detail with professionalism and heart.

What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive funeral and support services for families across KwaZulu-Natal and the Eastern Cape.

Repatriation Service

We handle the full repatriation process — ensuring your loved one is transported with care and dignity, wherever they need to go.

Funeral Cover Plans

Affordable funeral cover plans with no age limit. 4-month waiting period applies, with no waiting period for accidental death.

Décor for Any Event

Professional event décor services for funeral services, memorial ceremonies, and related gatherings.

Debit Order Facility

Flexible debit order payment options available, making it easy to keep your funeral cover active and up to date.

Cash Back Benefits

Selected plans include cash back benefits for main members and dependants to assist with funeral-related expenses.

Full Funeral Management

Caskets, tents, chairs, programmes, sound systems, fresh flowers, grave service, and audio obituaries — all handled for you.
